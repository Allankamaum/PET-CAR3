PET-CARE

Dog-Care

To take care of any dog you need to provide a protected and clean living environment for your dog.You also need to feed your dog a quality diet and prevent obesity.Have your pet checked reqularly by a veterinarian You can also communicate with your dog and develop a relationship and train your dog to follow the simple commands.Finally you should always take care of the dog's teeth,Dental care is very IMPORTANT,many breeds are prone to gum disease which can have serious implications.

Cat-Care

We recommend purchasing high-quality, brand-name kitten or cat food.Your veterinerian will be able to access your new cat or kitten and determinethe best diet.Cats require taurine,an essential amino-acid for heart and eye health.You will need to provide fresh,clean water ata all times, and wash and refill your cat's water bowls daily.Treats should be no more than 5-10% of the diet.Cats also need to be groomed,you should brush or comb your cat regularly,frequent brushing helps you keep your cat's coat clean,it reduces the amount of shedding and cuts down on the incidence of hairballs.Cats should have their own clean,dry place in your home to rest.To pick up your cat ,place one hand behind the front legs and another under the hindquarters,lift gently,never pick up a cat by the scruff of the neck or by the front legs.

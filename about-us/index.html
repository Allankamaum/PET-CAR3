ABOUT US

PetGuide is dedicated to covering what’s important to you as a pet parent, giving you all the information you need to ensure your pet lives a long, healthy and happy life. We’re pet parents, just like you, and we strive to deliver information that’s not only relevant to you and your pet, but easy to absorb and understand. Even better, all this information is in one place so it’s super easy to find exactly what you’re looking for.

Is it information of a dog you're in need of ? a cat maybe, or perhaps a fish or dog ? Then don't look no further than PetGuide To delight and educate – that’s our promise to you.

Giving you even more to wag about, PetGuide takes off the leash to bring you unbiased reviews and recommendations on the products and services you need for your pets. We’ve also enlisted the help of real pet owners to test out these dog products and services to ensure they meet your high standards.

PetGuideknows that pets are a part of your family. They offer unwavering loyalty, companionship and love 24/7. They don’t care if you’re having a bad hair day or you were late for work. You know you can count on your pet to be there for you when you get home, ready to smother you with love. With this in mind, we pledge to give you and your pet everything you need to enrich their lives as much as they enrich yours.
